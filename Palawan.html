    • Palawan

    A beautiful Place to live in a calming experience we should go thru.
    Palawan showcase Philippine's beautiful beaches and can offer you a great nature experience for the attractions in here are purely made by nature. With that I will show some places that you can enjoy the most
    of your stay here in Palawan.



    1 / 4
    El Nido
    El Nido is known for its stunning lagoons,
    white sand beaches, rocky islets, towering limestone cliffs, and glasslike waters teeming with marine life.
    2 / 4
    Kayangan Lake
    A crystal-clear freshwater lake in Coron,
    featuring underwater rock formations, caves and islets.


    3 / 4
    Honda Bay
    Numerous small islands are scattered in the bay,
    including Arrecife Island which is home to an upscale resort.
    4 / 4
    Barracuda Lake
    It is a freshwater lake taking place in a former crater.
    It is about 40m deep and surrounded by sharp limestone cliffs.
